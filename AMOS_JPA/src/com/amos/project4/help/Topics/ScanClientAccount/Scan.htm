How to Scan a Client Account

First, make sure you are connected to all Social Media Sites. If you are not connected, you will get a screenshot looking like this:

In that case, take a look at "How to connect to Social Media" here.

You can scan the database via the "Scan Social Media" button in the upper right corner.

The following window will open in which you can determine your search and what exactly should be searched for every user in the database.


If you have selected a single user (highlighted in blue, ), only his specific data will be searched and not the whole database.
